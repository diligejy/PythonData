KI Sung-Yueng will miss Swansea City's home match with Crystal Palace on Saturday after being ruled out for a week with concussion.

The South Korean midfielder was stretchered off just before half-time of Tuesday night's draw with West Brom following a clash with Stephane Sessegnon.

It left Ki sprawled on the pitch for several minutes before being carried off by medical staff.

But the problem is not as serious as first feared.
